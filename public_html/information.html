Travel

St. Dunstan's Church to Nonsuch Mansion

The walk from St. Dunstan's Church to Nonsuch Mansion takes about 25 minutes, just follow the crowd led by our handsome groomsmen. The walk is all on concrete paths but if you have heels then it may still be worth bringing some more comfortable footwear for the journey.


If the walk is not for you then then you can book a taxi with Cheam Station Cars by calling 0208 642 6400


Nonsuch Mansion to Holiday Inn Sutton

At the end of the evening there will be taxi's available at the mansion, both to go back to the Holiday Inn in Sutton or to any where else you are staying. Taxi's will be cash on the night at a competitive rate so make sure to have some cash with you.


Parking

The best place to park for St. Dunstan's Church is Cheam Library car park. The car park is pay and display so please remember to get a ticket.


You can park at Nonsuch Mansion for free during the reception and overnight.
